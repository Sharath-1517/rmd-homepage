Research and Innovation

The Research & Development (R&D) cell of R.M.D. Engineering College drives across boundaries to achieve Research Excellence through multiple disciplines. The Prime role is to create contacts with the real world and promote research through a range of publications and work to innovate and develop solutions that meet the present and emerging needs.It enables a pleasant environment for technological development and encourages the research activities of the College.
